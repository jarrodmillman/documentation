average_degree_connectivity¶

Examples

>>> G=nx.path_graph(4)
>>> G.edge[1][2]['weight'] = 3
>>> nx.k_nearest_neighbors(G)

>>> nx.k_nearest_neighbors(G, weight='weight')
{1: 2.0, 2: 1.75}
