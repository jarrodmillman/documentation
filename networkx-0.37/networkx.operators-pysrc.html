373  #    in graph theory. The fundamental issue is whether the names 
374  #    (labels) of the nodes (and edges) matter in deciding when two 
375  #    graphs are the same. For example, in problems of graph enumeration 
376  #    there is a distinct difference in techniques required when 
377  #    counting labeled vs. unlabeled graphs.
